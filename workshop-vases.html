WORKSHOP

Vases & Floral Design

4 hours  ·  Max 10 people  ·  All levels  ·  $280 per person

About This Workshop

An immersive half-day workshop bringing together two disciplines — ceramics and floral design — for a session that is as beautiful as what you'll make. In the first half, you'll work with Ebony to hand-build a sculptural vase: a striking, one-of-a-kind art piece designed to hold flowers or stand alone as a feature in your home.

In the second half, Eliza from Adelaide Flower House takes over, guiding you through a floral design session using seasonal blooms. You'll learn how to create designer arrangements at home — how to work with the form of a vessel, how to choose and place stems, and how ceramics and flowers elevate one another when considered together.

You'll leave with your fresh arrangement in hand, the skills to recreate it at home, and your vase will be fired and ready to collect in 5–6 weeks.

What to Expect

  • First half — hand-build one sculptural vase with Ebony: a one-of-a-kind art piece for the home
  • Second half — floral design with Eliza from Adelaide Flower House

  • Gain the skills to create considered arrangements at home
  • All clay, tools and flowers provided
