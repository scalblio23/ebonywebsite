WORKSHOP

Vases & Floral Design

4 hours  ·  Max 10 people  ·  All levels  ·  $280 per person

About This Workshop

A workshop for those drawn to both clay and flowers — two materials that share a quiet, organic beauty. Over four hours you'll hand-build one sculptural vase: a striking, one-of-a-kind art piece that holds its own in a room whether it carries flowers or stands alone.

This is a workshop about the relationship between a vessel and what it holds — how the form, the neck, the weight of a piece can elevate a single stem into something considered and deliberate. You'll work with Ebony to develop a vase that is genuinely sculptural, then arrange fresh seasonal flowers to explore how the two speak to one another. You'll leave with a handmade piece that changes the feeling of the space it sits in.

What to Expect

  • Hand-build one sculptural vase — a considered, stand-alone art piece for the home
  • Guidance on neck width, opening and form as they relate to flowers
  • A simple floral arrangement session using seasonal stems
  • Discussion of how ceramics and natural materials can shape a space
  • All clay, tools and flowers provided
  • Coffee and snacks provided
  • Vessel fired and ready for collection in 5–6 weeks
