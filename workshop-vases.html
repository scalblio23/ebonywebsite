WORKSHOP

Vases & Flower Arranging

4 hours  ·  Max 10 people  ·  All levels  ·  $280 per person

About This Workshop

An immersive half-day workshop bringing together two disciplines — ceramics and flower arranging — for a session that is as beautiful as what you'll make. In the first half, you'll work with Ebony to hand-build a sculptural vase: a striking, one-of-a-kind art piece designed to hold flowers or stand alone as a feature in your home.

In the second half, industry trained florist Eliza takes over, guiding you through a flower arranging session using seasonal blooms. You'll learn how to create designer arrangements at home — how to work with the form of a vessel, how to choose and place stems, and how ceramics and flowers elevate one another when considered together.

You'll leave with your fresh arrangement in hand, the skills to recreate it at home, and your vase will be fired and ready to collect in 5–6 weeks.

What to Expect

  • Gain the skills to create considered arrangements at home
