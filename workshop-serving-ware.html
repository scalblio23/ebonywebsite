WORKSHOP

Build Your Own Serving Ware

2.5 hours  ·  Max 10 people  ·  All levels  ·  $180 per person

About This Workshop

Designed for those who love beautiful things on the table, this workshop is about scaling up your forms — moving beyond small vessels into larger, more considered pieces. You'll be guided through creating 1–2 handmade serving pieces: platters, boards, bowls or dip dishes that are as functional as they are considered.

Working with slabs and hand-building techniques suited to larger scale work, you'll develop an understanding of how clay behaves differently as your forms grow. Ebony will guide you through decisions of form, proportion and surface so each piece feels intentional and distinctly yours.

What to Expect

  • Introduction to slab building techniques for larger-scale forms
  • Create 1–2 serving pieces across the session
  • Learn how to scale up your forms while maintaining strength and integrity
  • Guidance on proportion, rim detail and surface texture
  • All clay and tools provided
  • Pieces fired and ready for collection within 5–6 weeks
