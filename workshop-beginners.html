WORKSHOP

Beginners Hand-building

2 hours  ·  Max 10 people  ·  All levels  ·  $98 per person

About This Workshop

This is the perfect introduction to the world of ceramics — and the perfect excuse to get a group together. No experience needed, just curiosity and a willingness to get your hands dirty. In this relaxed, social two-hour session you'll learn the fundamentals of hand-building using pinch and coil techniques, two of the oldest and most intuitive ways of working with clay.

Popular with friends, work groups and special events, this workshop has an easy, unhurried energy. The focus is on enjoying the process, connecting with the material, and leaving with something genuinely handmade. A brilliant way to spend an afternoon with people you like.

What to Expect

  • A warm welcome and introduction to the studio and materials
  • Guided pinch and coil techniques — building small to medium vessels from scratch
  • Each person will make 2–3 vessels across the session
  • Texture and surface exploration
  • All clay and tools provided — just wear something you don't mind getting messy
  • Your pieces will be fired and available for collection within 5–6 weeks
