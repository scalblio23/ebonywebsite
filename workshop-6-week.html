About This Course

A six-week journey into hand-building ceramics, designed for those who want to develop a genuine practice rather than a one-off experience. Each week builds on the last — from foundational pinch and coil techniques through to slab construction, surface treatment and glaze.

Kept to a maximum of eight participants, this course allows for real progress, meaningful feedback and a growing confidence with the material. By the end you'll have a body of work and the skills to keep going.

What to Expect

  • Week 1 — Introduction to clay: pinch and coil fundamentals
  • Week 2 — Form and function: building vessels with intention
  • Week 3 — Slab construction: plates, bowls and flat work
  • Week 4 — Independent project: apply your skills to create your own piece
  • Week 5 — Decorating: carving, stamping and surface treatment
  • Week 6 — Glaze your own pieces
  • All materials and firings included across the 6 weeks
