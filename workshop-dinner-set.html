WORKSHOP

Build Your Own Dinner Set

4 hours  ·  Max 5 people  ·  Beginner–Intermediate  ·  $420 per person

About This Workshop

An immersive half-day workshop for those who want to build something truly meaningful — a complete, handmade dinner set that will earn its place on your table for years to come. Using slab construction and slump moulds, you'll work through 4 dinner plates, 4 side plates and 4 pasta bowls, developing a cohesive set that reflects your own aesthetic sensibility.

Kept to just five participants, there's space here for real focus — on consistency of form, on the decisions that make a set feel like a set, and on the quiet satisfaction of making something functional and beautiful with your own hands. No prior experience needed.

What to Expect

  • Slab rolling and slump mould techniques for plates and bowls
  • Build 4 dinner plates, 4 side plates and 4 pasta bowls
  • Guidance on achieving consistency of form, thickness and proportion across a set
  • Discussion of glaze options and surface finishes to suit your aesthetic
  • Personalised attention throughout — maximum 5 participants
  • Coffee and snacks provided
  • All materials included
  • Pieces glazed and fired, ready for collection in 5–6 weeks
